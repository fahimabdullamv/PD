About Me

Designing for clarity,
not complexity.

I'm a product designer with 5+ years of experience shipping digital products across mobile and web. My background spans consumer apps, B2B SaaS, analytics dashboards, and e-commerce — always focused on making complex systems feel simple and obvious.

My process is grounded in research, systems thinking, and close collaboration with engineers and PMs. I believe good design is inseparable from good product thinking — equally at home in a Figma file, a user interview, a sprint review, or a stakeholder presentation.

I've been deepening my skills in AI-augmented design workflows — using Figma Make, Cursor, and LLM-based synthesis to move faster without sacrificing depth.
